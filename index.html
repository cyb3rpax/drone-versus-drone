THE RULES OF THE SKY

Simple to learn. Unforgiving to ignore.
  1. Pilot account required. Register or log in every session — your coins, fleet and record belong to your account.
  2. One battery per leg. It drains the whole flight, and one charge covers only ~60% of the round trip. Watch the range estimate.
  3. Recharge on pads only. Run dry over open water and you ditch at sea — mission failed.
  4. The round trip pays, not the halfway point. Harmony, top up, home to The Cove — then bank your 🪙100.
  5. Terrain wins every argument. Hard hits past your airframe's tolerance break a part.
  6. Broken drones limp. A fraction of your speed, treetop height — just enough to reach the parts shop.
  7. You fix it yourself. Buy the part (credit if you're broke), drag each screw to its mount, torque it with the arrows.
  8. Spend wisely. Upgrades, skins and airframes are permanent on your account.
